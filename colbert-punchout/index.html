Stephen Colbert's Magical Punchout WHAT?! Adventure (2008)

Authors Mathew Mason, Andrea Pollitt, Michael Prinke

Description 

Members:

Mathew Mason: Lead Programmer
Andrea Pollitt: Lead Artist, Level Design
Michael Prinke: Artist, Character Design, Story

Platform/Requirements: DirectX, .NET 2.0 Framework

To skip the story completely, hit Enter. To go forwards or backwards in the story, use the arrow keys.

Known Issues:

Overworld Collision:
	The overworld collision was turned off because there were 
	multiple places where the player would get stuck,
	and we didn't have enough time to fix it completely.
