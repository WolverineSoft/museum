Michigan Beirut

          This game was written in C using HAM, a gba library. The graphics were created in inkscape and paint, and downsampled for the gba. 
          
          The game is meant to be simple and addictive.
        
The game is a simulation of the popular drinking game beirut (beerpong). You must find the best combination of angle and power in order to land your ball into your opponent’s cups. After landing a ball in a cup, that cup will disappear, if you lose all your cups, you lose the game. If all of your opponent’s cups disappear, you win. Each player alternates throwing the ball.

The angle pointer and power meter will move on their own, use the A button to lock each, one at a time.
