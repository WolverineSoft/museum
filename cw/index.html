Simple Controls:
Enter or Space - Select in menu screens, open chests, open doors, sit on
bus, talk to people, Etc...
Esc, 'x', 'c', 'v', 'b', 'n' - cancel menu options, and access menu screen.

f4 - minimize or maximize the window.
f5 - Minimize even further f4-ed window.
f12 - restart game to menu screen (WITH NO SAVE!!!)

QUICK HINTS: Save often. 
              Use potions in and between battles. 
        Buy a lot of magic replenishing items, they really help.
              Use Tech Skills, they can really turn the battle around.
              Remember to equip all weapons/amour in the menu screen right after
                  you get them.
