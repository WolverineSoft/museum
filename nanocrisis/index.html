Controls:
Move with A,S,W,D or the arrowkeys.
Use J and K to attack and jump, or use control and command.
Q and E will rotate the camera in some araeas.

Controls cannot currently be changed, but if it is requested I may add this feature.

You can press control+space to enter fullscreen mode.  640x480 currently is the only mode.  This may change later.
