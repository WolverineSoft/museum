Tube Fighter XII: Hussain Awakens (March 2007)

Authors Jason Eaton, Paul Skowronek, and Jason Skorski

Description PETA has assaulted University research facilities and freed dozens of monkeys. These monkeys have gathered thousands more monkeys, slain PETA, and taken up residence in the bowels of the CSE building. Our intrepid President awakens to the sound of monkeys - you must FIGHT through the monkeys, through the depths of the CSE building, and through the jaws of death itself.
