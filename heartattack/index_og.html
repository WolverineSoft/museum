Heart Attack!

About

Heart Attack is a goofy action arcade game for the PC. It is a simple game that is meant to be played with an analog joystick (though can be played from the keyboard). Its highlights are single and multiplayer modes, simple addictive gameplay, and cool music. I am using this game as test-bed for the 2D portion of my Real Time Fluid Dynamics direct study.

Requirements

Heart Attack requires DirectX 9c and a gamepad with an analog joystick is highly recommended. Heart Attack is still very much in a beta stage and has not yet been optimized. You can press F1 in the game to reveal your frames per second if you do this I would greatly appreciate feedback on your machine's performance (please include system specs).
