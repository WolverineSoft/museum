Granicus (2007)

Authors Andrew Pinson

Description A two-player space battle game 

Improvements since 48 hour contest:

Support for keyboard controls for players 1 and 2.
Non-configurable joystick support for player 1.
Lines no longer draw farther than their maximum length.
Lines disappear immediately if there are too many lines on the screen.

Lines turn green if they can be placed, red if they cannot (if there are 5 lines on the screen).
Sounds for line hit, ship hit, and ship destroy.
        
Player 1

Xbox 360 controller as shown on the title screen. (Note that "A" causes a quick rotation)

Keyboard control:
Move: W A S D
Rotate: G and H
Fire: Space Bar

Joystick/joypad control (results may vary):
Move: Joystick 1 (or Buttons 13-16, mapped to a D-pad in the order "left", "up", "right", "down")
Rotate: Joystick 2
Quick rotate: Button 1
Fire blue: Button 11
Fire green: Button 12

(This was mapped to an original Xbox controller in the same manner as the 360 controller)
------------
Player 2

Xbox 360 controller as shown on the title screen. (Note that "A" causes a quick rotation)

Keyboard control:
Move: O K L ;
Rotate: Left and Right arrow keys
Fire: Ctrl
------------
Line player

Left click and drag to draw line
