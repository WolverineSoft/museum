Revenge & Reform (2019)

Authors voxlap, Amber Renton

Description

Revenge and Reform is a multi character platformer in which you take on various forms of Wolvey the Wolverine to take out baddies. 



Use the ever adorable Smolvey's roll ability to move past spikes!



Or Spellvey's handy projectile attacks to keep enemies at bay!



Find keys scattered about the levels to unlock mysterious doors!
