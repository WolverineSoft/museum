Saving Grace (2020)

Authors gracewqma

Description

All UofM students have been quarantined! Play as Sawyer from Michigan Tech as he travels to Ann Arbor to save Grace from quarantine. Explore and battle your way down the state of Michigan.

Unzip the folder and run the executable. Used mouse and keyboard. WASD to move, mouse to interact, Shift to sprint.
